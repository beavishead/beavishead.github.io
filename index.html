The website for finding the similar images to the one uploaded by the user. Uses the Vision Transformer by google under the hood. The dataset of ~10000 images is first passed through the Transformer's layers, the last hidden state is then used as a feature representation of the images. Then being compared to the uploaded image. Website provides top 6 similar matches via cosine similarity. All wrapped up in the Flask app for easier user interaction.
